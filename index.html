The Pipeline

Four stages. One platform. Zero glue code.

Euxine ships the entire analytical pipeline as a single, integrated system. No stitching together twelve open-source projects. No writing Terraform for three months before seeing your first dashboard.

  • Ingest — Kafka-native connectors for databases, APIs, files, events
  • Process — SQL-first transformations, escape to code when needed
  • Store — columnar OLAP optimized for fast aggregations
  • Visualize — embedded dashboards, self-serve exploration

Sovereignty

Your data never leaves your jurisdiction.

Deploy on any cloud, any region, or bare metal. Euxine doesn't phone home. There's no control plane in our cloud. Your data stays exactly where you put it.

  • Any cloud — AWS, Azure, GCP, Hetzner, OVH, bare metal
  • Any region — deploy where your compliance requires
  • No phone home — zero telemetry to our servers
